# Formations: 3-4-3 Family (Wing-Back / Three-at-the-Back)

Formations using three centre-backs with wing-backs providing width defensively and offensively.

---

## 3-4-3
**Line groups**: Defense (CB, CB, CB) | Mid (LWB, CM, CM, RWB) | Attack (LW, ST, RW)
**Positions**: GK · CB · CB · CB · LWB · CM · CM · RWB · LW · ST · RW
**Strengths**: Very attacking three-pronged attack; wing-backs provide defensive cover; narrow front three presses effectively
**Weaknesses**: Defensively risky; wide spaces conceded when wing-backs advance; requires elite wing-backs

## 3-4-2-1
**Line groups**: Defense (CB, CB, CB) | Mid (LWB, CM, CM, RWB) | Att-Mid (CAM, CAM) | Attack (ST)
**Positions**: GK · CB · CB · CB · LWB · CM · CM · RWB · CAM · CAM · ST
**Strengths**: Excellent against midfield-heavy teams; flexible with wing-back and attacking mid shifts; strong possession base
**Weaknesses**: Wing-backs require exceptional speed and stamina; pivot demands high activity; steep learning curve

## 3-4-1-2
**Line groups**: Defense (CB, CB, CB) | Mid (LWB, CM, CM, RWB) | Att-Mid (CAM) | Attack (ST, ST)
**Positions**: GK · CB · CB · CB · LWB · CM · CM · RWB · CAM · ST · ST
**Strengths**: Balanced shape; CAM links midfield to attack; dual strikers work in tandem; flexible between back 4 and 5
**Weaknesses**: Only width from wing-backs; high squad depth demands; space behind CAM can be exploited

## 3-2-4-1
**Line groups**: Defense (CB, CB, CB) | Def-Mid (CDM, CDM) | Mid (RM, CM, CM, LM) | Attack (ST)
**Positions**: GK · CB · CB · CB · CDM · CDM · RM · CM · CM · LM · ST
**Strengths**: Eight players through the middle create massive central dominance; quick counter-press capability; facilitates aggressive pressing
**Weaknesses**: Wide CBs must be comfortable on the ball; striker faces constant pressure; vulnerable if attacking mids are marked tightly
